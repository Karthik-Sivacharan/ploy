## What Ploy Is Building

Marketing has become too fragmented across tools and teams. Ploy is pulling everything together by combining a company's brand, website, and multi-channel growth into a single AI-native platform. The goal is to give marketing and sales teams one place where everything works without friction.

### The Market Gap

Ploy would be the first platform to unify all three in an AI-native way:

| Capability | Current Tools (Fragmented) |
|---|---|
| Brand management | Frontify, Bynder |
| Website building | Webflow, Framer, WordPress |
| Multi-channel growth | HubSpot, Jasper, point solutions |

No current player bridges all three as AI-native. HubSpot comes closest but is AI-enhanced (not native) with a weak website builder. Webflow is great at sites but not a marketing platform. Jasper handles content but doesn't build websites.

## Prototype Direction

Given what Ploy is building, a prototype could showcase:
- A **unified dashboard** where brand assets, website pages, and campaign channels live together
- An **AI-native workflow** — e.g., "create a campaign" that auto-generates on-brand landing page + email + social posts from a single brief
- Clean, opinionated design that contrasts with HubSpot's complexity
